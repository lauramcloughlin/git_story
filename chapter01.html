The Traditional Sunday Dinner

The Sunday dinner, there are many things you need to consider when planning to make a great Sunday dinner.

  • How many will be at your dinner?
  • Deciding what to cook e.g. the roast
  • Will you have the time to do all the shopping and prep work?

Some useful cooking instructions to help.....

The roast...

Roast Beef

Ingredients - 1kg beef - top rump or topside joint (1kg will feed four), 1 tsp mustard powder, 1 tsp plain flour

Method - Heat the oven to 240C/220C fan/gas 9. Mix the flour and mustard powder with salt and pepper and rub into the beef joint. Place the beef into the roasting tin and cook for 20 mins. Then reduce oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 5 and continue to cook the beef for 30 mins if you like it rare, 40 mins for medium and 1 hr for well done. Remove the beef from the oven and allow the beef rest for at least 30 minutes and carve into slices for serving.

Roast Chicken

Ingredients - 1.8-2kg or 4lb-4lb 8oz chicken, butter

Method - Heat oven to 220C/200C fan/gas 7. Put the chicken in a large roasting tin. Rub the outside of the chicken with butter. Season inside and out, then cook the chicken for 20 mins. Turn the oven down to 190C/170C fan/gas 5 and cook for a further 45 mins or until the chicken juices run clear when you pierce with a knife. Leave to rest for 15 mins before carving.

Other options for your Sunday roast include lamb, pork and turkey.
